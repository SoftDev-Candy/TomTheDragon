**About the Game**
Tom The Dragon is a light-hearted platformer aimed at kids aged 7â€“11. You play as Tom, a curious little dragon trying to escape from a rising sea of lava. The idea is simple: keep climbing higher while collecting power-ups and avoiding obstacles.

During the climb, players can:
Bounce on mushrooms for extra height
Dodge rocks thrown by mole miners
Use blue flowers for speed boosts
Use yellow flowers for super jumps
Explore colorful, friendly environments
The higher you climb, the more intense (and fun) it gets!
